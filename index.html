About

I'm Arshdeep Kaur

I'm Arshdeep Kaur. Born on 15 September 2025, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Applied Health Science in Human and Social Studies at Wilfrid Laurier University in Canada. I am currently in my third year of studies. During my time at the university, I have immersed myself in various learning experiences that have shaped my understanding of public health.
Serving on the Health Studies Student Association executive has strengthened my teamwork and event planning abilities. Job shadowing at SOAR Family Chiropractic exposed me to patient care in a clinical environment. Through hands-on labs, simulated projects, and community service placements, I have gained practical skills and confidence in communicating with diverse groups. These experiences fuel my passion for promoting wellness and supporting others on their health journeys.
